Sambar and rice

Ingrediants:

  • 3 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, capsicum, cauliflower), finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 4-5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter for cooking
  • Pav buns
  • Chopped onions and coriander leaves for garnishing
  • Lemon wedges

Recipe:

  1. Heat butter in a pan. Add minced garlic and grated ginger. Sauté for a minute.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and pulpy.
  4. Add chopped mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender.
  5. Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add mashed potatoes and mix until everything is combined.
  7. Use a masher to further mash the mixture until it's well blended and smooth.
  8. Adjust the consistency with water if needed. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  9. Toast the pav buns with butter on a griddle until they're slightly crispy.
  10. Serve the hot bhaji with toasted pav buns, garnished with chopped onions, coriander leaves, and lemon wedges.
