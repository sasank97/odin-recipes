Sambar and rice

Ingrediants:

  • 1/2 cup toor dal (pigeon pea lentils)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 potato, diced
  • 1/2 cup chopped mixed vegetables (beans, drumsticks, etc.)
  • 2 tablespoons sambar powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Tamarind pulp (size of a small lime)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water
  • Coriander leaves for garnishing

Recipe:

  1. Pressure cook the toor dal until soft and mushy. Set aside.
  2. In a pot, heat oil and add chopped onions. Sauté until they turn golden.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  4. Add diced vegetables, sambar powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Sauté for a few minutes.
  5. Add tamarind pulp and enough water. Cover and let it simmer until the vegetables are cooked.
  6. Add the cooked toor dal and mix well. Adjust the consistency by adding more water if needed.
  7. Simmer for a few more minutes. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
